About Me

I was born in Japan and raised in California. After high school I joined the Marine Corps and proudly served four years. Post Marines I studied marketing in college and stumbled into advertising, media planning specifically. I've worked in advertising for 17 years in various capacities and have seen a dramatic change in the landscape.

I have enrolled in the Coding Bootcamp at the UCLA Extension to to get a complete understanding of web development. I am eager to learn and apply this skillset to my current role as well as prepare me for the future. I am looking forward to this journey!
